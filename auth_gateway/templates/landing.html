Platform Capabilities

Everything You Need to Analyze Funds

Four powerful analytical modules covering every dimension of mutual fund intelligence.

Market Overview

Track macro-level insights: total AUM distribution across fund houses, industry folio growth, SIP inflow milestones, and category-wise net flows in real time.

Performance & Risk

Evaluate true fund quality with institutional metrics: Alpha, Beta, Sharpe & Sortino ratios, Maximum Drawdowns, VaR, and CVaR computations.

Investor Demographics

Deep-dive into investor behavior. Track age-income matrices, SIP vs. Lumpsum preferences, KYC compliance, and Tier-1 vs. Tier-2 city penetration.

Portfolio Holdings

Look under the hood of any fund. Interactive Sunburst charts for sector-to-stock exposure, top 10 holdings by weight, and HHI concentration scoring.
